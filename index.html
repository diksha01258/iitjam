IIT JAM Exam Pattern 2024, Marking Scheme and Question Pattern

IIT JAM Exam Pattern 2024 comprises seven papers: Mathematical Statistics, Biotechnology, Geology, Chemistry, Economics, Mathematics, and Physics. Explore the IIT JAM Exam Pattern 2024 for detailed test structure and content insights.


IIT Madras is the organizing institute for this year’s JAM exam. Aspiring candidates must adhere to the eligibility criteria while completing the application form. This information provides a comprehensive overview of IIT JAM 2024, including the detailed exam pattern.

HIGHLIGHTS





IIT JAM 2024 - The Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, has actvated the IIT JAM 2024 mock tests links on its official website. Candidates can attempt IIT JAM mock test online using default login and password. The Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, will open the IIT JAM 2024 application correction window on November 20, 2023. The candidates will be able to access the login window and make corrections in the student particulars of the IIT JAM application form 2024 at the official website. The Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, will publish the IIT JAM admit card 2024 on January 8, 2024, in online mode.... Candidates can download the IIT JAM 2024 admit card by visiting the JOAPS 2024 candidate portal and by entering their login details. The IIT Madras closed the IIT JAM registration 2024 on October 25. The registered candidates for IIT JAM 2024 can make the payment till October 27. Indian Institute of Technology, Madras started the IIT JAM 2024 registration on September 5 in online mode. Candidates could apply for the IIT JAM registration 2024 by visiting the official website of IIT Madras -jam.iitm.ac.in. The IIT Madras will conduct the IIT JAM 2024 exam to offer admissions to M.Sc programmes this year. Candidates could register for the exam by filling and submitting the IIT JAM 2024 application form before the deadline. The IIT Madras released the IIT JAM dates on its official website. IIT Madras will release the JAM 2024 answer key on the official website. Indian Institute of Technology, Madras will declare the JAM 2024 result on March 22 on its official website. Candidates can raise objections to the IIT JAM answer key during the IIT JAM 2024 answer key challenge window.




  • Fully objective type, with three types of questions: (i) Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ), (ii) Multiple Select Questions (MSQ), and (iii) Numerical Answer Type (NAT) questions.
  • Candidates can appear for either ONE or TWO test papers.
  • DIRECT admission to over 3000 seats in various postgraduate programmes at IITs.
  • JAM 2023 Scores to be used for admissions to over 2300 seats by various CFTIs including NITs, IISc, DIAT, IIEST, IISER Pune, IISER Bhopal, IIPE, JNCASR, SLIET
